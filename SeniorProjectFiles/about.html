What am I looking at?

An experiment! This is Invisible Ink, an experiment done by some Computer Science students and headed by a member of the SIUE English Department.

Learn more »

What is Ephemeral Art?

Ephemeral art is any piece of art made specfically with the intent of the artwork not staying around for too long. Think carved ice sculptures.

Learn more »

Who is the Artist?

Everyone! Anyone! The artist can be anyone. Following the "Submit" link you can submit literary work to the admin of this website and given some time it will be uploaded and be put through the algorithms.

How is it done?

Each story is first stored on a server then, given certain parameters will be degraded via a custom built algorithm developed for this project.

Over time, every story will degrade till nothing remains.

Can I contribute?

Yes! If you follow the "Submit" button you will be able to submit.
